About.

Background.

I’m a second year student at the University of Wolverhampton expecting to graduate in September 2020.

As a software engineer, I enjoy taking ideas and engineering solutions and fixes to problems that I am presented with, using my technical knowledge to deliver the best product that I can. After I have completed a task I will normally talk to my peers to see if they came up with a different solution to help further my knowledge.

When I’m not coding - you can probably find me watching a football game, reading about the latest scientific theory or researching about our history.
